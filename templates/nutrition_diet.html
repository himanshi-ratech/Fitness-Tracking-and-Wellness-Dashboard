Proteins

Build and repair tissues, crucial for muscle growth and recovery. Found in pulses, eggs, paneer, and meat.

Carbohydrates

Primary energy source; choose complex carbs like oats, brown rice, and whole grains for sustained energy.

Fats

Support hormone function and nutrient absorption. Include healthy fats like nuts, seeds, and olive oil.

Fiber

Improves digestion and keeps you full. Found in vegetables, fruits, and whole grains.

Vitamins & Minerals

Support immunity, energy production, and bone health. Eat colorful fruits and vegetables daily.
